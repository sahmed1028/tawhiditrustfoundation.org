About Us

Tawhidi Trust Foundation is a non-profit organization dedicated to helping poor and needy families in the global Ummah (Islamic community).

Following the values of Rahmah (Mercy) and Khidmah (Service), we work to bring hope, dignity, and support to people facing hardship.

Our Mission

Our mission is to support vulnerable families through food assistance, clean water projects, medical help, shelter support, and charitable programs in Bangladesh.

"The best people are those who benefit others."

Our Projects

  • 🍚 Food Aid (It’am) — Food packages and meals for families in need
  • 💧 Clean Water (Sadaqah Jariyah) — Water projects for communities
  • 🏠 Shelter & Clothing — Support for basic living needs
  • 🩺 Medical Help (Shifa) — Medicine and healthcare assistance
  • 🤲 Zakat & Sadaqah — Helping those who need support
  • 📚 Education and emergency assistance as needs arise
